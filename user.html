Operating systems can also be found at the palm of our hands. Phones, tablets, MP3 players also run a type of Operating System. Some run iOS while others run Android. But even though they are similar operating systems to that a desktop or laptop, the operating system of theses devices are not as equipped as a desktop or laptop.


Each device and even the laptop and desktops run applications. You might wonder what exactly is an application?
An application is the software that allows for a device to perform a specific task.
